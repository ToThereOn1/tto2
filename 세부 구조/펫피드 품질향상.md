## 근본 원인 분석 (Architect)

프롬프트 지시 간 **우선순위 모호성**이 근본 원인.
현재 프롬프트에 동시에 존재하는 지시들:
1. `FIRST SENTENCE FORMAT (mandatory)` — letterCausality에서
2. `USE DIRECTIONAL CONTRAST in your opening` — todaysDrive에서
3. `Start differently every time` — system prompt OPENING에서
4. `OPENING BAN: Your last entry started with...` — narrativeAnchors에서
5. `Reference at least one thing from recent events` — World Bible에서

→ LLM이 5개 지시를 동시에 만족하려 "안전한 중간 지대"로 수렴. 인과성이 희미해짐.

**해결 방향**: 새 지시 추가(X) → 기존 템플릿 문장을 **더 구체적인 예시로 교체**(O)

### 변경 1: just_received — FIRST SENTENCE FORMAT + ECHO FORMAT 교체

**적용 위치**: `buildLetterCausalitySection()` → `just_received` 분기, 대략 line 282-283

**문제**: 편지가 행동 방향을 바꿨다는 건 나타나지만, 편지의 '무엇'(단어/이미지)과 원래 계획이 보이지 않음

**OLD:**
```
→ FIRST SENTENCE FORMAT (mandatory): "The letter sent me [direction] instead of [where I was heading]." OR "I'd been heading [X] — then [guardianLabel]'s words turned me [Y]." Show the direction change, not the letter content.
→ ECHO FORMAT (use once mid-post): "${guardianLabel} used to [notice/worry about something specific about you]. Here, that same thing [shows up differently / finally makes sense / still matters]."
```

**NEW:**
```
→ FIRST SENTENCE FORMAT (mandatory): "The letter sent me [specific place] instead of [specific planned destination]. I'd planned [concrete activity] — but [one phrase from the letter] changed the route." Show BOTH the original plan AND the new direction. Be concrete: name the place, the path, the terrain.
→ ECHO FORMAT (use once mid-post): Show a reflex ${guardianLabel} would recognize — not by naming ${guardianLabel}, but by doing the thing: a head-tilt, a paw-check, a pause at a specific texture. The action IS the echo. One sentence. No explanation.
```

**기대 효과**: 인과 점수 +2~3점 (19→21+)
**토큰 증가**: +5

---

### 변경 2: still_carrying — OPENING FORMAT 구체화

**적용 위치**: `buildLetterCausalitySection()` → `still_carrying` 분기, OPENING FORMAT 줄, 대략 line 307

**문제**: "140시간 전 편지가 오늘 행동에 영향"이 독자에게 보이지 않음 — "implied but not explicit" 반복 판정

**OLD:**
```
→ OPENING FORMAT: "[what you did] instead of [what you would have done before the letter]. [That was new.] OR [I wouldn't have done that yesterday.]" — This BEFORE/AFTER contrast in the first sentence is how the judge sees letter impact.
```

**NEW:**
```
→ OPENING FORMAT: "[specific physical action at specific place] instead of [specific habitual action]. [That was new / I wouldn't have done that two days ago.]" — Name the PLACE and the BODY PART. Example pattern: "I stayed at [X] instead of [habitual Y]. My [body part] [specific motion]." The judge scores higher when terrain and body are both concrete.
```

**기대 효과**: still_carrying 인과 점수 +2점
**토큰 증가**: +9

---

### 변경 3: daily_routine — "심화" 경로 추가 (healingDirection 분기)

**적용 위치**: `buildTodaysDriveSection()` → `daily_routine` case → non-closure fallback (line 345)

**문제**: Rex처럼 routine이 강한 캐릭터에게 매일 "이탈하라"고 요구 → 캐릭터 정체성 모순, 이탈 이유 막연

**OLD:**
```typescript
return `\n▸ WHY TODAY → FIRST SENTENCE: This is a routine — but today one small thing sent you a different direction. Name the CAUSE and the shift: "[A sound/scent/pull] sent me to [X] instead of [Y] today." Not just the difference — name what triggered it. ${contrastHint}`;
```

**NEW:**
```typescript
return healingDirection
    ? `\n▸ WHY TODAY → FIRST SENTENCE: The routine ran as always — but today you went deeper into it instead of away. Something inside the familiar pulled you further. Name what you noticed for the first time in a place you've been a hundred times: "[At X], I [noticed/felt/caught] [specific detail] that I'd walked past every day until now." The routine is the container. The discovery is the story. ${contrastHint}`
    : `\n▸ WHY TODAY → FIRST SENTENCE: This is a routine — but today one small thing sent you a different direction. Name the CAUSE and the shift: "[A sound/scent/pull] sent me to [X] instead of [Y] today." Not just the difference — name what triggered it. ${contrastHint}`;
```

**주의**: `closure` early return(line 342-343)은 이 분기 이전에 위치 — 충돌 없음. `healingDirection` 파라미터는 이미 함수 시그니처에 optional로 존재.

**기대 효과**: healingDirection 있는 루틴 캐릭터의 인과 + 세계관 체감 +2점
**토큰 증가**: 실제 프롬프트 기준 +0~15 (ternary 한쪽만 실행)

### 변경 4: NPC WORLD MOMENT — RETURNING 분기 "NPC acts, pet notices" 구조

**적용 위치**: `buildStatusFeedPrompt()` → NPC WORLD MOMENT 섹션, RETURNING 분기 내부 (line 760-762)

**문제**: NPC가 "Cloud was on the far side", "Wind was near the edge" 식의 배경 소품으로 등장 → 세계관 체감 18-19에 머묾

**OLD:**
```typescript
    : `→ RETURNING: ${petName} has met ${localNpcName} before. No introduction needed. Show familiarity through small behavioral cues — a glance of recognition, a familiar position, a routine they have already settled into.`
}
→ NPC WORLD MOMENT: ${localNpcName} does ONE specific small action for themselves during this event — something with a clear purpose of their own. Show the action, not the purpose. One phrase: "[NPC name] was [specific action] — [brief sensory detail]." This makes ${localNpcName} feel like a real inhabitant with their own life, not a prop.
```

**NEW:**
```typescript
    : `→ RETURNING: ${petName} has met ${localNpcName} before. No introduction needed. Show familiarity through small behavioral cues — a glance of recognition, a familiar position, a routine they have already settled into.
   SUBJECT FLEXIBILITY: In RETURNING encounters, ${localNpcName} may be the subject of UP TO TWO sentences — but only to show their independent action, not to describe their personality.`
}
→ NPC WORLD MOMENT: ${localNpcName} does ONE specific small action for themselves during this event — something with a clear purpose of their own. Show the action, not the purpose. One phrase: "${localNpcName} was [specific action] — [brief sensory detail]." Then show how ${petName} reacted to it — a glance, a shift, a pause. Two beats: NPC acts, pet notices.
```

**주의**: FIRST MEETING 분기(line 755-758)의 SUBJECT LOCK은 별도 ternary 분기 — 물리적으로 영향 없음(Architect 확인)

**기대 효과**: 세계관 체감 +1~2점 (NPC RETURNING 시나리오)
**토큰 증가**: +22
