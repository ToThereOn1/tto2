# 07. 시간 엔진 & 학습 단계 시스템

## 개요

ToThereOn의 시간은 지구와 다른 비율로 흐르며,
펫의 글쓰기 능력은 시간에 따라 7단계로 진화합니다.

---

## 시간 엔진 (time-engine.ts)

### 핵심 공식

```
TIME_RATIO = 3  (지구 3일 = ToThereOn 1일)

earthDaysPassed = (now - passedDate) / (1000 × 60 × 60 × 24)
toThereOnDaysPassed = earthDaysPassed / TIME_RATIO
currentDay = max(1, floor(toThereOnDaysPassed) + 1)
progress = toThereOnDaysPassed - floor(toThereOnDaysPassed)  // 0~1 소수
```

### 시간 예시

| 지구 시간 | ToThereOn Day |
|-----------|---------------|
| 3일 | Day 1 |
| 6일 | Day 2 |
| 9일 | Day 3 |
| 21일 (3주) | Day 7 |
| 90일 (3개월) | Day 30 |
| 300일 (~10개월) | Day 100 |
| 1년 | ~Day 122 |
| 3년 | ~Day 365 |
| 50년 | ~Day 6,083 |

### 시간 오프셋


## 7단계 학습 시스템 (learning-stage.ts)

### 단계 정의

| 단계 | Day 범위 | 이름 | typoRate | vocabRichness | 폰트 | 길이 배수 |
|------|----------|------|----------|---------------|------|-----------|
| Stage 1 | 0-3 | First Light | 0.80 | 0.10 | Gaegu | 0.4x |
| Stage 2 | 4-6 | Budding Voice | 0.60 | 0.25 | Gaegu | 0.6x |
| Stage 3 | 7-9 | Growing Words | 0.35 | 0.45 | Nanum Pen | 0.8x |
| Stage 4 | 10-11 | Finding Flow | 0.15 | 0.65 | Nanum Pen | 1.0x |
| Stage 5 | 12-66 | Clear Expression ⭐ | 0.05 | 0.85 | Nanum Pen | 1.3x |
| Stage 6 | 67-166 | Poetic Soul | 0.00 | 0.95 | Nanum Myeongjo | 1.3x |
| Stage 7 | 167+ | Timeless Voice | 0.00 | 1.00 | Nanum Myeongjo | 1.3x |

### 단계별 글쓰기 지침

#### Stage 1: First Light (Day 0-3)
```
- VERY short, broken sentences (2-5 words max)
- Many deliberate typos and misspellings
- Very simple, repetitive vocabulary
- Strong emotion with minimal words
- Example: "i mis you. tail wag. today gud. you come soon?"
```

#### Stage 2: Budding Voice (Day 4-6)
```
- Short, broken sentences (3-7 words)
- Noticeable typos and misspellings
- Growing but still basic vocabulary
- Starting to form short phrases
- Example: "i went to the medow today. the buterflis are prety."
```

#### Stage 3: Growing Words (Day 7-9)
```
- Short-to-medium sentences, mostly correct
- Some typos but improving
- Expanding vocabulary
- Sentences flow but grammar imperfect
- Example: "today I runned in the garden. the flowers smelled nice."
```

#### Stage 4: Finding Flow (Day 10-11)
```
- Medium sentences with mostly correct grammar
- Very few typos remaining
- Good vocabulary, simple but expressive
- Clear structure with natural expression
- Example: "I found a quiet spot by the lake today."
```

#### Stage 5: Clear Expression (Day 12-66) ⭐ MASTERY
```
- Full, emotionally warm sentences
- No typos; clear and fluent writing
- Express feelings, memories, observations with clarity
- Example: "The afternoon light fell softly through the leaves today,
            and I thought of you..."
```

#### Stage 6: Poetic Soul (Day 67-166)
```
- Elegant, nuanced expression with emotional depth
- Rich, varied vocabulary with poetic touches
- Weave memories and feelings with literary grace
- Example: "There is a particular quality to the light here in the evenings
            — amber and forgiving — that reminds me of the kitchen window..."
```

#### Stage 7: Timeless Voice (Day 167+)
```
- Transcendent beauty and deep wisdom
- Masterful vocabulary, profound emotional intelligence
- Every word chosen with care, layers of meaning
- Example: "I have learned that missing someone is not an absence
            but a presence — a warmth that lives in the bones..."
```

## 학습 단계의 영향 범위

```
learning-stage.ts (순수 유틸)
    ├─→ reply-generator.ts
    │     ├─ 시스템 프롬프트의 LANGUAGE LEARNING STAGE 섹션
    │     ├─ 답장 길이 결정 (stage multiplier)
    │     └─ dynamicMaxTokens 결정
    │
    ├─→ event-generator.ts
    │     ├─ 학습 이벤트 타입 결정 (observation/lesson/practice)
    │     └─ 프롬프트에 학습 가이드라인 섹션 삽입
    │
    ├─→ LetterPaper.tsx (UI)
    │     ├─ 폰트 클래스 (gaegu/nanum-pen/nanum-myeongjo)
    │     └─ 레이아웃 스타일 (fontSize, lineHeight, letterSpacing)
    │
    └─→ writing-mastery.ts (DB 연동)
          └─ pets.passed_date → ToThereOn Day → Learning Stage
```
